About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Tufts Zoo, where our journey began over half a century ago in the heart of our community. What started as a family-run sanctuary has blossomed into a beacon of animal conservation and education.

Our Mission

At Tufts Zoo, our mission is to inspire a love for nature and wildlife in every visitor. We're committed to providing a safe haven for endangered species and to fostering a deep understanding of the precious balance within our ecosystem.

Our Commitment to Conservation

Conservation is not just a concept here; it's our way of life. We actively participate in international breeding programs, contribute to scientific research, and work tirelessly to protect animal habitats around the globe.

Experience the Wild

We invite you to explore our diverse array of exhibits, which are designed to give you a glimpse into the wild worlds of the animals we care for. From the African savannahs to the Amazon rainforests, embark on an adventure that will take you around the globe.

Join Us

Whether you're a lifelong animal lover or a curious newcomer, we welcome you to join us on this journey. Your visit, support, and enthusiasm help us to continue our critical work in conservation. We can't wait to share our passion with you!
