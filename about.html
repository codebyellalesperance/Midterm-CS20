About Us

Our History

The Tufts Zoo was established in 1950 with the goal of providing a sanctuary for endangered species. Over the course of the last 74 years, we have welcomed over 10 million people.

Our Mission

At the Tufts Zoo, our mission is to promote wildlife conservation, provide education about endangered species, and offer unforgettable experiences for our visitors.

Our Team

Our team of dedicated professionals includes zoologists, veterinarians, educators, and conservationists who work tirelessly to ensure the health and well-being of our animal residents.

Conservation Efforts

We actively participate in conservation programs aimed at protecting endangered species and their habitats. This ensures that you have a good time, and so do our animals.
