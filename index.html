Welcome to Tufts Zoo!

Join us for an unforgettable adventure into the wild.
At Tufts Zoo, we pride ourselves on creating harmonious connections between our visitors and the many remarkable species that call our grounds home.

At the Tufts Zoo, our mission is to promote wildlife conservation, provide education about endangered species,
and offer unforgettable experiences for our visitors.

Established in 1950, Tufts Zoo aimed to create a refuge for endangered species.
In the past 74 years, it has opened its doors to more than 10 million visitors.
