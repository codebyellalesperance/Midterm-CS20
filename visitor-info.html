Rain Policy

Tufts Zoo is open rain or shine, but certain sections of the zoo may be closed or close early on rainy days.

All daytime Zoo tickets are rain-or-shine, and we don't offer refunds, rainchecks or rescheduling.

We encourage you to dress accordingly, or check out our gift shop for rain gear during your visit.

Parking

Parking is FREE at the Tufts Zoo

Parking for the zoo is located in the large lot across the street from our main entrance.

Handicap Parking is accessible next to employee parking in the front entrance lot.
