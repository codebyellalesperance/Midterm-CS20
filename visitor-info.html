Rain Policy

Tufts Zoo is open rain or shine, but certain sections of the zoo may be closed or close early on rainy days.

All daytime Zoo tickets are rain-or-shine, and we don't offer refunds, rainchecks or rescheduling.

We encourage you to dress accordingly, or check out our gift shop for rain gear during your visit.

Visitor Guidelines

1. Respect the animals.

2. Follow the instructions of the staff at all times.

3. Be kind and respectful to other visitors.

4. Specific exhibits may have different guidelines posted.

5. Wash your hands often.

6. Bags may be briefly checked at the entrance.

7. No outide food is allowed.

8. Photography is allowed and encouraged.

9. Do not throw objects or food into an animal enclosure.

10. Children must be supervised at all times.

11. Refrain from running at the zoo.

12. Pets are not allowed.

13. Do not try to touch the animals.

14. Guests must wear proper attire.

15. Alchohol and Smoking is absolutely not permitted.

Failure to be in compliance with the rules above may lead to immediate removal from the zoo grounds.

Parking

Parking is FREE at the Tufts Zoo

Parking for the zoo is located in the large lot across the street from our main entrance.

Handicap Parking is accessible next to employee parking in the front entrance lot.
